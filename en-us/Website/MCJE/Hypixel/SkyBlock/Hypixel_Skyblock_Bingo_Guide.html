Hypixel Skyblock Bingo Guide (Based on Personal Opinion Only)

Money Making Method for Bingo

Early Game
  1. Mining Sand and sold to Shop
    2. Requirement: Gold Shovel with Efficent V Enchantment
    Income: +20,480 per 64 Enchanted Sand
    This method is good for early game, you could make up the money you needed by mining sand.
    It is not recommended for large amount of money because it could be very tired after mining sand in a long period.

  2. Finding Relic in Spider's Den
    3. Requirement: Enough Speed
    Income: +10,000 per Relic found. (Maximum amount: +280,000 for 28 Relic found)
    This method is good if you have someone to help you kill the spider so that you can touch the Relic.
    It is not recommended if you do not have someone to help, you will be easily killed by Spider.

Mid-Game
  1. Killing Goblin
    2. Requirement: Dreadload Sword, Enough Mana
    Optional Requirement: Mana IV potion, Server with no people killing goblin in your area.
    Income: Unstable, Usually +100,000 per Mana IV potion consumed
    This method is good for mid-game, goblin can drop a bag of coin, their gear and egg can been sold to shop for money too.
    Tip: To obtain Mana IV potion, player can first find and mine Glowing Endstone in The End. Add a Glowstone Dust in Mana III potion, Restone Dust also recommended.
